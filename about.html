Founder of Dreambia Convention Center

Dreambia Convention Center proudly introduced the largest event space in Bangladesh. We offer the most reasonable price of Hall Rent, Decoration & Food at Nikunja, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Founded in 2018, Dreambia Convention Center is one of the largest and leading Convention Center in Bangladesh. With the purpose and determination of assuring uncompromising quality, the Dreambia Convention Center has grown to be an excellent service oriented establishment, diversified to accommodate a specialized wide range of corporate, social and wedding needs for up to 3000 guests within 4 separate floor together. Many weddings & social events have been brought to life here .
